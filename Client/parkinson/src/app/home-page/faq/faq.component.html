Frequently Asked Questions


What is Vocalson?

It is an application to predict whether a person is diagnosed with Parkinson's or not.


How to use Vocalson?

First add a patient to the system then input his/her five recordings of voice. Finally the system will predict the result


How accurate are voice analyzing tests?

It is not 100% accurate. If the patient gets the positive results, its better to take other Parkinsons's related tests.


How long will it take to predict the result?

It won't take that much, the results will be shown within 1-2 minutes
